Chitwan

Chitwan National Park is the first national park of Nepal and has been granted the status of World Heritage. The national park consists of 68 species of mammals with the most popular being the Bengal tiger. The amazing feeling of subtropical forests during Elephant and Jungle safari are worth visiting Chitwan for. Activities like Canoeing, Safari, sunsets, and cultural programmes are some of the most eventful experience that Chitwan provides.
